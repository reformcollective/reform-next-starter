How It Helps

Multi-Surface Monitoring

Take a proactive approach to security, with tools to monitor domains, ports, IP addresses, and certificates for potential vulnerabilities before they become a problem

  • Domain Monitoring

    Monitor all your organization's domains and subdomains for misconfigurations, spoofing attempts, and subdomain takeovers, with host names, IP addresses, sources, and active/inactive status on every domain.

  • Port Monitoring

    Unsecured ports are a prime target for cybercriminals. The Hub continuously scans critical entry points—such as web servers and databases—for vulnerabilities. Real-time alerts helps identify and close risky open ports before they can be exploited.

  • IP Monitoring

    Track IP addresses that interact with your digital assets. The Hub monitors for potentially malicious activity, providing detailed data on IP origin, host name, and port usage. Stay vigilant with constant surveillance of suspicious IPs.

  • Certificate Monitoring

    Ensure your SSL/TLS certificates are up to date and correctly configured. The Hub monitors certificate expiration dates and provides proactive alerts, helping prevent potential disruptions or vulnerabilities due to expired or misconfigured certificates.
